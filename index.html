Our Story

The journey from village kitchens to your plate

Bringing Village Flavors to the City

Mana Orru Ruchulu started with a simple dream - to preserve and share the authentic flavors of village cuisine with the world. Our founder, Smt. Lakshmi, grew up in a small village where meals were cooked on wood fires in clay pots, using ingredients grown in their own backyard.

When she moved to the city, she missed those flavors and the warmth of village cooking. In 2005, she opened a small eatery serving dishes from her childhood, prepared the traditional way. The response was overwhelming, and over the years, Mana Orru Ruchulu has grown into a beloved restaurant while maintaining the authenticity of village cooking.

Today, we continue to serve dishes prepared with recipes passed down through generations. We source our ingredients from local farmers and our own organic garden, ensuring the flavors remain as authentic as possible.
